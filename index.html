How it works

1. Import

Drag in your Claude or ChatGPT exports. Velqua extracts personal facts, filters noise, deduplicates.

2. Learn

Every conversation through the proxy is scanned for new facts. Quality scored. Automatically stored.

3. Inject

Relevant facts are injected into every request. Your AI knows you without you repeating yourself.

4. Coordinate

Pro tier adds multi-agent mesh. Shared memory pools. Agent identity. Real-time dashboard.
