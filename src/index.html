Step 3

How Velqua works

The proxy adds memory automatically, so your clients do not need custom prompt code.

1 Your app

Sends a normal chat request to Velqua on localhost.

→
2 Velqua

Retrieves facts and episodes, then injects them into the request.

→
3 Your provider

Receives the enriched prompt and returns the final response.

Why this matters

You keep your existing tools, but they become memory-aware because Velqua is the layer in between.
